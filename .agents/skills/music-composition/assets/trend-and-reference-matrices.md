## Evidence-confidence labels

| Label | Meaning | How to answer |
|---|---|---|
| High confidence | Multiple sources and references agree | Give concrete advice, still note scope |
| Medium confidence | One strong source plus listening evidence | Give advice as a likely direction |
| Low confidence | One reference, vague tag, or platform-only signal | Be cautious; ask for reference or web-check |
| Conflicting signal | Platforms or regions disagree | Explain which signal the advice optimizes for |
